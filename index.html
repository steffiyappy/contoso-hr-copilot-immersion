EXERCISE 1 0:05 – 0:25 · 20 min 📄 Employee_Handbook.docx + Leave_Policy.docx + Benefits_Guide.docx HR Director

HR Policy Q&A with Copilot Chat

Goal: Use Copilot Chat with attached HR documents to answer employee policy questions per Employment Act 1955 (2022 amendments). Eliminate manual HR calls with instant self-service.

Wow moment: Manual HR calls → Instant self-service. 30-minute wait → 30-second answer.

Task 1 — Answer policy questions ☁️ Copilot Chat (Work)

Open Copilot Chat. Attach Employee_Handbook.docx and Leave_Policy.docx. Then use this prompt:

I've attached our Employee Handbook and Leave Policy. Please answer per Employment Act 1955 (2022 amendments): (1) Maternity leave entitlement (EA 1955: 98 days) (2) Paternity leave (EA 1955: 7 days) (3) How to apply for Flexible Working Arrangement under S.60P? (4) What is the maximum working hours (EA 1955: 45 hrs/week)?
Task 2 — Drill down on statutory benefits ☁️ Copilot Chat (Work)

Continue the same conversation (do NOT start a new chat). Attach Benefits_Guide.docx and ask:

Based on the Benefits Guide, explain Malaysian statutory requirements: (1) EPF contribution rates — employer 13%/12%, employee 11% (2) SOCSO new ceiling RM6,000 (effective October 2024) (3) EIS 0.2% contribution — verified? (4) Minimum wage compliance RM1,700 (effective February 2025) Are we above or below each statutory minimum?
Task 3 — Create employee FAQ ☁️ Copilot Chat (Work)

Continue the same conversation:

Create an Employee FAQ covering Malaysian employment law — top 20 questions on: - EA 1955 leave entitlements (annual, sick, maternity, paternity) - EPF/SOCSO/EIS contributions and when deductions apply - Flexible Working Arrangement procedures (S.60P) - Notice periods (S.12) and overtime rates (S.60A: 1.5× normal rate) Format as Q&A with short plain-language answers suitable for employees.
EXERCISE 2 0:25 – 0:45 · 20 min 📄 Sample_CV_1.docx + Sample_CV_2.docx + Sample_CV_3.docx + Job_Description.docx Talent Acquisition

CV-JD Screening & Matching

Goal: Screen CVs against a Job Description ensuring non-discriminatory practices per EA 1955 S.60L (no discrimination based on pregnancy, gender, religion, or marital status).

Wow moment: 45 mins/CV manual review → 10 mins for all 3 CVs with instant scoring and interview questions.

Task 1 — Screen CVs against JD ☁️ Copilot Chat (Work)

Start a new Copilot Chat. Attach all 3 CVs and the Job_Description.docx. Use this prompt:

Compare these 3 CVs against this Job Description. Per EA 1955 S.60L fair hiring: (1) Assess skills and qualifications only — no age/gender/marital bias (2) Verify Malaysian qualifications (MQA accredited institutions) (3) Score alignment with JD requirements: 1–10 (4) Recommend suitability without discriminatory criteria Provide a summary for each candidate.
Task 2 — Generate unique interview questions per candidate ☁️ Copilot Chat (Work)

Continue the same conversation:

Generate 3 unique interview questions for each candidate — targeting their specific gaps identified in the screening. IMPORTANT: Avoid EA 1955 S.60L prohibited topics. Do NOT ask about: - Pregnancy plans or family planning - Marital status or number of children - Ethnicity, religion, or nationality beyond work eligibility Focus on: skills verification, experience depth, and technical competency.
Task 3 — Create comparison matrix ☁️ Copilot Chat (Work)

Continue the same conversation:

Create a comparison matrix compliant with Malaysian fair hiring practices: Columns: Candidate Name | MQA Qualification | Skills Match % | Experience Fit | JD Alignment Score | Recommendation Note: Matrix must NOT include columns for age, gender, marital status, or personal status — EA 1955 S.60L compliance required. Format as a clear table ready for the hiring panel.

Tips & Quick PromptsGet the most out of Copilot Chat for HR

Quick-start prompts

Check EA 1955 Leave Entitlements
What are the current leave entitlements under Employment Act 1955 (2022 amendments)? Include: annual leave by tenure, sick leave, maternity (98 days), paternity (7 days), hospitalisation, and emergency leave provisions.
Minimum Wages Quick Check
What is the current minimum wage in Malaysia under the Minimum Wages Order 2024? When did RM1,700 take effect? Are there any exceptions for small employers (<5 employees)?
EPF/SOCSO/EIS Rate Check
Confirm the current statutory contribution rates in Malaysia: EPF employer and employee percentages, SOCSO monthly wage ceiling (note: RM6,000 effective October 2024), and EIS 0.2% contribution. Are there any pending changes?
IRA 1967 Domestic Inquiry Checklist
Provide a step-by-step Domestic Inquiry (DI) checklist per Industrial Relations Act 1967 Malaysian requirements. Include: Show Cause Letter procedures, DI panel composition rules, employee rights (natural justice), documentation requirements, and timeline guidelines to avoid S.20 unfair dismissal claims.

Copilot Chat tips for HR

  • Attach multiple files at once — Copilot Chat can reason across all attached documents simultaneously.
  • Be specific about legal references — include the Act name and section (e.g., "per EA 1955 S.60L") for more accurate responses.
  • Use follow-up prompts — "Drill down on point 3" or "Reformat as a table" without re-uploading files.
  • Ask for citations — add "provide source citations" or "reference the relevant legislation" to ground responses.
  • Specify output format — "Format as a table", "Write as a policy document", "List as bullet points".
  • Bilingual outputs — add "Provide in both Bahasa Malaysia and English" for bilingual HR communications.
Always verify: Copilot Chat may not have the latest amendments to Malaysian employment law. Always verify statutory rates (EPF, SOCSO, minimum wage) and court decisions against official government sources: Ministry of Human Resources (MOHR), KWSP/EPF, PERKESO/SOCSO.
